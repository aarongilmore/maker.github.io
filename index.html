MAKER.

Maker is an independently published magazine about how people are using the web to help others. It focuses on organisations and non-profits that are using the Internet to build communities, share stories, and raise money with the aim of effecting areas such as healthcare and education.

Each issue will consist of four long-form interviews exlporing why these individuals do what they do and will discuss the advantages and disadvantages of a world that relies so heavily on technology.

Maker will be published quarterly and will ship worldwide. Sign up below to stay up-to-date.
